Phase 1
Foundation
Days 1–30
Build vocabulary in each discipline. Survey the landscape. Identify what excites you most. No deep dives yet — breadth before depth.
Phase 2
Connection
Days 31–60
Start noticing patterns across disciplines. A principle in physics mirrors one in music theory. Historical events echo in today's headlines. Begin weekly projects.
Phase 3
Synthesis
Days 61–90
Produce original work that stacks skills. Create projects that only you — with your unique combination of disciplines — could make.
